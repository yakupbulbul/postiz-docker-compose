Privacy Policy

SocialQ

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SocialQ ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use SocialQ at socialq.cloud. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Data TypePurpose
Email addressAccount creation and authentication
Password (hashed)Secure account access
Social media OAuth tokensPublishing content on your behalf
Social media profile infoDisplaying connected accounts
Post content and mediaScheduling and publishing your posts
Usage dataService improvement and troubleshooting

3. Third-Party Platform Data

When you connect social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and others), we access only the data necessary to provide our service:

  • TikTok: We use TikTok's Login Kit for authentication and Content Posting API for publishing videos. We access your TikTok display name and profile picture for account identification. We do not access your private messages, followers list, or viewing history.
  • Other platforms: Similar limited OAuth access for publishing content and retrieving basic profile information.

OAuth tokens are stored securely and are used exclusively to perform actions you initiate (such as publishing a scheduled post). You can revoke access at any time by disconnecting the account in Settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the social media scheduling service
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Publish content to connected platforms on your behalf at scheduled times
  • Send email notifications about post status (success, failure, streak reminders) if enabled
  • Improve service reliability and fix issues

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement the following security measures:

  • Passwords are cryptographically hashed and never stored in plain text
  • OAuth tokens are stored securely in an encrypted database
  • All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access to servers is restricted and protected

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With social media platforms when you explicitly request to publish content
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or prevent fraud

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we remove your personal data, OAuth tokens, and post content from our systems. Some data may be retained in backups for a limited period.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the service
  • Disconnect any linked social media account at any time
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of email notifications via Settings
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the effective date of the latest revision below. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

For questions or requests regarding your privacy, contact us at support@socialq.cloud.
